## Lee v. Weisman, 505 U.S. 577 (1992)

https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/505/577/

### Summary

#### **Primary Holding**

The Establishment Clause does not permit a public school to hold a religious prayer led by clergy during its graduation.
